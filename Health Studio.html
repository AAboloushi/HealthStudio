WHEN WE SAY HEALTY, IT IS HEALTHY!

Health Studio Restaurant & Cafe is a locally owned food business serving healthy foods, snacks and beverages. Driven by his passion for cooking healthy delicious meals, Chef Yousef Ashkanani, a renowned Kuwaiti Chef, Blogger, established the company in 2017 along with a group of Kuwaiti businessmen who share the same passion for food. Combining Chef Yousef’s long years of experience in the field of health and nutrition and his partners enterpreneurial skills, Health Studio Restaurant & Cafe continues to grow and became known to fitness enthusiasts. A 100% Kuwaiti owned company, Health Studio Restaurant & Cafe established its reputation to local and foreign nationals as one of the growing companies serving healthy meals cooked and prepared upholding Kuwaiti tradition.

About Health Studio

The company aims to top the market locally and franchise across the Middle East. Health Studio started to expand its operation through intensive marketing in Cooperatives such as Mishref, Faiha, Adeliya, and Qurtoba. The company aims to serve more Cooperatives in Kuwait to reach out to every local and foreign health enthusiasts. Health Studio products are made available in Vortex Café and Frosting Treats. The company has tied up with local service provider, Carriage, for customers who wish to have their healthy meals delivered at the comfort of their home. Customers can get freshly cooked meals, detox, juices, well prepared Grab and Go meals as well as other menus with less hassle and the same amount of meal freshness and nutritional value.

Health Studio Restaurant & Cafe continues to develop healthy menus giving attention to food quality, freshness and nutrition value targeted to fast-food consumers. Meals ranging from variety of chicken, beef, seafood and vegetable menus. Stiff competition among healthy restaurants encourage Health Studio to be the friendliest and trendiest joint in Kuwait.

The company caters to more than 250 members in all parts of Kuwait through its Monthly Membership Meal Plan. Membership meals are served to fit customer preferences. Take away meals are also made available at the main store in Jabriya providing freshly made ready-to-eat meals at a very affordable price.
